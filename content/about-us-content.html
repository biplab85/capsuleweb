Who We Are

We're a Sydney-based digital marketing team that started with one simple frustration: too many businesses were paying agencies a lot of money and getting very little in return. Vague reports, recycled strategies, and locked-in contracts that served the agency, not the client.

Capsule Digital was built to do the opposite. We work with businesses who are tired of guessing whether their marketing is actually working. We keep things honest, we keep things clear, and we only stick around if we're delivering results you can see.

Why We Exist

Most small and mid-sized businesses don't need a massive agency with layers of account managers and generic playbooks. They need a team that actually sits down, understands how their business makes money, and builds a plan around that.

That's what we do. We exist because we believe growing your business online shouldn't require a six-month contract, a confusing dashboard, or a marketing degree to understand your own reports.

How We Work

Every client gets a dedicated account manager. Not a chatbot, not a rotating team of juniors, not someone you have to chase for updates. One person who knows your business, picks up the phone, and tells you the truth even when the numbers aren't pretty.

We're based in Sydney, which means we're available for face-to-face meetings when you need them. But more importantly, it means we understand the market you're operating in, the customers you're trying to reach, and what it actually takes to compete locally.

What We Believe In

No lock-in contracts. If we're not delivering, you should be free to walk away. We'd rather earn your trust every single month than hold you to a piece of paper.

Transparent pricing with no hidden fees. You'll know exactly what you're paying for and exactly what you're getting before we start.

Reports that make sense. We don't hide behind vanity metrics or jargon-filled dashboards. If something's working, you'll know. If something's not, you'll know that too, along with what we're doing to fix it.

Who We Work With

We work with businesses across e-commerce, healthcare, real estate, hospitality, fitness, SaaS, and professional services. But the common thread isn't the industry. It's the mindset. Our best clients are the ones who are serious about growth, open to doing things differently, and ready to invest in marketing that actually moves the needle.

Whether you're just getting started online or you've been burned by a previous agency and want to try again with a team that actually cares, we'd like to have that conversation with you.
