[ Who We Are ]
01

We're a Sydney-based digital marketing team built on one frustration.

Too many businesses were paying agencies a lot of money and getting very little in return. Vague reports, recycled strategies, and locked-in contracts that served the agency — not the client.

Capsule Digital was built to do the opposite. We work with businesses who are tired of guessing whether their marketing is actually working. We keep things honest, we keep things clear, and we only stick around if we're delivering results you can see.

Small businesses don't need massive agencies with generic playbooks.

They need a team that actually sits down, understands how their business makes money, and builds a plan around that.

We exist because we believe growing your business online shouldn't require a six-month contract, a confusing dashboard, or a marketing degree to understand your own reports.

[ How We Work ]
03

Every client gets a dedicated account manager. One person who knows your business.

Not a chatbot, not a rotating team of juniors, not someone you have to chase for updates. One person who picks up the phone and tells you the truth — even when the numbers aren't pretty.

We're based in Sydney, which means we're available for face-to-face meetings. But more importantly, it means we understand the market you're operating in, the customers you're trying to reach, and what it actually takes to compete locally.

We work with businesses serious about growth.

E-commerce, healthcare, real estate, hospitality, fitness, SaaS, and professional services. The common thread isn't the industry — it's the mindset. Our best clients are open to doing things differently and ready to invest in marketing that actually moves the needle.

Whether you're just getting started online or you've been burned by a previous agency and want to try again with a team that actually cares — we'd like to have that conversation with you.

[ What We Believe In ]

Principles that drive every decision we make.

No lock-in contracts. If we're not delivering, you should be free to walk away. We'd rather earn your trust every single month.

Transparent pricing with no hidden fees. You'll know exactly what you're paying for and exactly what you're getting before we start.

Reports that make sense. We don't hide behind vanity metrics. If something's working, you'll know. If it's not, you'll know that too.
